ABOUT CODING COMPETITION

The purpose of the coding competition is to motivate students towards coding and enhance their coding skill, increase competitiveness, improve time management and prepared themselves for future challenges in industry and other allied field. Institute is planning to organize this type of competition for students of all semesters at least two times in a year. Visit this site regularly for getting updates.
